BRANDS

Maxon

Maxon Infrastructure Private Limited was founded in 2013 under the name Max Decors. In 2019, we introduced two new segments, Maxon Console and Max Interior, which played a pivotal role in the company's evolution into Maxon Infrastructure Private Limited. Previously operating as a non-private limited entity, the company underwent a transformation and is now a private limited company. During this transition, we successfully acquired trademarks for both Maxon and Max Decors.Maxon Infrastructure, under the visionary leadership of CEO Nazeer, is more than just a manufacturer – we are creators of Modular Kitchen, Wardrobe, Loft, Pooja unit, Wall Panelling, TV Unit, Zebra Blinds, Roller Blinds, Roman Blinds, and Wallpaper solutionsy.

Windows and Walls

"Windows and Walls Interior Designs" has proudly served our community as a Certified Hunter Douglas dealer since 1997. As a locally owned and operated business, we have dedicated ourselves to enhancing the beauty and functionality of homes and offices through our exquisite range of blinds, drapery, and drapery hardware. With nearly three decades of experience, our team of experts is committed to providing top-notch design solutions and premium products to transform any space into a masterpiece of style and comfort. Whether you're looking for window treatments that offer privacy, light control, or a touch of elegance, trust us to deliver the perfect solution tailored to your unique needs and preferences.

ACTION TESA

The Action Group, one of India’s largest business conglomerates, was established in the early 1970s under the guidance of Shri N. K. Aggarwal, a respected leader, visionary, and philanthropist. Its journey began with Action Shoes, a footwear brand that quickly gained popularity and became a household name. Driven by a spirit of continuous growth, innovation, and a commitment to excellence, the group has diversified its operations across multiple sectors including footwear, electronics, real estate, chemicals, flex manufacturing, coal mining, and engineered wood panel products. From unique lighting fixtures to stylish furniture and captivating decor, Craft Looks provides a one-stop destination for those seeking to infuse their spaces with a touch of modern sophistication.

Kitply Industries

Kitply Industries Limited is India’s foremost manufacturer of Plywood and Blockboard. It was founded by Shri. S.P.Goenka and Shri. P.K.Goenka in 1982 in Assam, India. Kitply’s operations are spread throughout the country with 5 factories and 16 Sales Offices. Having a workforce of about 1000+ employees, it caters to a countrywide distribution network of over 300+ dealers. In India, Kitply’s brand name is synonymous with plywood and enjoys a high level of equity in the Indian market. It pioneered the concept of “Branded Plyboards” in the early 80’s. The company’s products occupy an enviable position in the Indian marketplace and have multifarious applications – Ranging from household to buildings to construction. It is the only plywood company that features in the list of Consumer Super Brands of India.

Greenply Industries

Greenply Industries, India's leading plywood manufacturer, has long been synonymous with excellence in the woodworking industry. Their extensive product portfolio is a testament to their commitment to serving a diverse range of needs and demands. Whether you're looking for plywood solutions for construction, interior design, or furniture, Greenply has you covered with an unparalleled variety of high-quality options. Among their standout offerings is the Shuttering Grade plywood, recognized for its robustness and reliability, making it a preferred choice for construction projects across the country. Greenply's unwavering dedication to quality and innovation has solidified its position as the undisputed leader in the Indian plywood market, setting industry standards and redefining what's possible with wood.

Ebco

Since its inception in 1963 as a humble Tool Room, Ebco has grown to become a pioneering force in the Indian furniture hardware industry. Over the years, Ebco has undergone a remarkable transformation, diversifying its offerings and expanding its product range. Today, it proudly holds the esteemed position of being India's leading manufacturer in this domain. Ebco's journey to the top has been characterized by a strong foundation in manufacturing, a commitment to maintaining stringent quality standards, a dedication to affordability, and an extensive all-India distribution network. These strengths collectively define Ebco's legacy and set it apart as a trusted name in the industry. Ebco's success is a testament to its adaptability, innovation, and unwavering focus on delivering quality products to its customers.
